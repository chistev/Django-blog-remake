Sean 'Diddy' Combs: Singer Cassie accuses rap mogul of rape and abuse

By Chistev Staff • November 17, 2023 • 8 min read

Cassie, whose full name is Casandra Ventura, has filed a lawsuit against Sean "Diddy" Combs accusing the music mogul of rape, physical abuse, and emotional manipulation spanning several years.

According to court documents filed in New York, the singer claims the abuse began shortly after she started dating Combs in 2007 when she was 19 years old. The lawsuit alleges repeated incidents of sexual assault and physical violence.

The Allegations

The lawsuit details multiple disturbing incidents, including an alleged rape in 2018 after Cassie attempted to leave the relationship. Cassie claims Combs subjected her to years of control, including forcing her to participate in sexual activities with others while he watched.

"I hope that by sharing my story, I can help others who have faced similar situations and encourage them to speak up." — Cassie Ventura

Combs' representatives have denied all allegations, calling them "baseless and meritless." The music executive has not yet issued a formal public statement regarding the lawsuit.

Impact on the Music Industry

This case has sent shockwaves through the hip-hop and entertainment industry. Many artists and industry insiders have begun sharing their own experiences, calling for greater accountability and reform in how power dynamics are handled in the music business.

The lawsuit comes at a time when the industry is already facing increased scrutiny over sexual misconduct and abuse of power following several high-profile cases in recent years.
